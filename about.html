About



Plan My Path was created by Grace Cantarella, a current junior at Stuyvesant High School in New York City.

Throughout Grace's first two years of high school, she noticed that she had trouble planning too far in advance. Even keeping track of daily tasks became a struggle as her workload grew exponentially.

She came up with the idea for Plan My Path when she began touring colleges the summer before Junior year. She didn't know where to start, and wished there was a singular place where she could find all the resources she needed in order to plan and lay out her future. And thus, Plan My Path was born.

You can contact Grace with any questions at gracecantarella14@gmail.com. Happy planning!
